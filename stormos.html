About Storm OS

 Storm OS is a Linux distro based on Arch developed by Ben Fitzpatrick with the contributions by silent robot, seeker, rayzer1981, yugiohmaster88, and more.

Included software

  •  Steam
  • Discord
  • Wine
  • Lutris
  • Gimp
  • Libreoffice
  • Zoom

And much more!
